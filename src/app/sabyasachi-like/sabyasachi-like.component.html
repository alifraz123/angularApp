SABYASACHI

Sabyasachi Mukherjee started his eponymous label in 1999, with a workforce of three. Today, he retails from five flagship stores around the country and employs about 1100 people directly while outsourcing work to another 35000 people. Every Sabyasachi piece is lovingly, painstakingly and deliberately created by skilled artisans using the highest degree of craftsmanship, quality and devotion.
